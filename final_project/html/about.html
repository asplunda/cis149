About MMO Trend

MMO Trend is a hobbysite I have created to discuss one of my favorite activities... MMO's! MMO is an acronym that stands for Massively Multiplayer Online game. Though the most common Type of MMO is an RPG(role playing game), over the years other genres have dabbled in the concept. A major genre that has become a more common type of MMO is FPS(first person shooter). As time goes on, developers are beginning to prove that it's possible for just about any genre to try it's hand at MMO!.
