History

Origins

The general consensus of what the first game that started the MMO genre was Ultima Online in 1997. Most MMO's to follow modelled themselves after concepts that were introduced by Ultima Online. Two years after the success of Ultima Online, Everquest released and was a major success and dominated the market for near six years. Following these games Asheron's Call was released in the wake of their success and these three games together are credited as the original MMO's that launched the genre to it's incredible future.
