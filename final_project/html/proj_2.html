Project 2: Wildstar

Wildstar is a new MMO from Carbine Studios featuring very involved combat. Unlike in many traditional MMO's with the "stand here, cast this" style of combat, Wildstar has a very active combat system. Players will need to be moving constantly avoiding the attacks of the enemies while aiming their own. Many abilities allow for channeling while moving, further emphasizing the high mobility of the game.
