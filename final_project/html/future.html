Future

The MMO market has changed significantly since it's birth with nearly all MMO's using a free-to-play business model. Attempting to release a subscription based MMO now has proven to be devastating to a company leading them to switch to free-to-play models shortly after releasing. Mostly due to the dominance of World of Warcraft. Despite its success, players are beginning to move more towards the free-to-play MMO's. The future seems to see MMO's moving almost exclusively to a free-to-play genre as a whole using in-game shops to turn profits.
