Glossary

There are many terms used in MMO games that mostly consist of acronyms to make communication in game smoother when not using a voice-chat program. I will cover the more commonly used terms as an introduction to what you can expect.

  • PST - Please Send Tell or whisper in game
  • LFG - Looking for Group
  • WTS - Want to Sell
  • WTB - Want to Buy
  • Toon/Char - Your character in game
  • Mob - Monsters or enemies in game
