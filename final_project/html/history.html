History

MMO's have been around for a long time now. Knowing where they came from and the journey they have taken to get to the point they are at now isn't really essential knowledge, but it's interesting to know. They have become much more user friendly than they used to be and as such has turned them into a monstrous force in the gaming industry and one of the most popular types of games.
