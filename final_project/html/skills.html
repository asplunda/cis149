Skills

The skills required for playing an MMO are relatively minimal. They are designed in a way that most anyone can jump in and have fun playing them. In order to get far though, requires a decent amount of patience and willingness to put in the time to progress.

Playing an MMO can help you develop some life skills in a bit of an obscure way. Participating in difficult max level content of same games gives you a lot of time to work on teamwork and communication under pressure. Sounds a bit strange but these abilities really do come into play.
