FAQ

Q: What is a good MMO to start out with?
A: The most user friendly and easiest MMO to get into in my opinion has definately got to be World of Warcraft. You just can't go wrong there.

Q: Do I need a high end computer to play MMO's?
A: Not really no. A lot of MMO's are tailored to run on an extremely wide variety of machines and have very customizable graphics settings to suit most PC's.

Q: Should I play a free-to-play, or subscription MMO?
A: It depends on your preference. Many free-to-play MMO's have a lot of content locked or restricted unless you spend money. Whereas subscription based MMO's give you unlimited access to everything available in the game because of your subscription.
