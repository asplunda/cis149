Class Mastery

One of the first decisions you will make in your MMO adventure is the class that you will play. When you first begin, it might be a good idea to play each of the classes available so that you can get a feel for how they all work and find the style that fits you the best. Once you find the class that you can identify yourself with, LEARN EVERYTHING! The equipment you will use, the skills you need to use, the professions that best suit you, all of it. Mastering your class and being able to show that you know what you are doing will earn your place in the group once you reach the max level. Nothing is worse for players than trying to teach someone how to play their character when they are already at max level. Make it easier on everyone and educate yourself!
