Project 1: EQ Next

Everquest Next is a new game in the Everquest franchise that will put the classic titan of the MMO industry into the next generation set of games. The game is emphasizing on a ton of player involvement in the development of the entire world itself through the use of their Landmark program. In Everquest Next: Landmark, players are able to gather resources and build incredible structures on their own personal plots of land in the world and other players can come see their creations. Some of the best creations may also end up being put into the world of Everquest Next itself!
